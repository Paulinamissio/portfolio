Usability Testing

Usability testing is a great way for users to test out your app and recieve feedback. A great way to see if you have solved the problem at hand, or how to improve. Before moving onto the final prototype.

Overall this app is targeted at users who are interested in what's going on in their school. I decided to go with a less intense design by using minimal colours and a simple layout. Keeping the app minimal and simple helps make it easier and fun to use
