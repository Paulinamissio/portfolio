STUDENTPOWR
UI/UX, Branding

Studentpowr is an app that allows students to better connect with their student body. The challenge was that most people didn't know where student council posted events, and they thought their voices weren't being heard.

THE SOLUTION

This app gives the student council a platform to communicate with their student body. Students can also participate in voting polls, and provide valuable feedback to help improve the student council.
