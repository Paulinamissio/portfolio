GARDENLIFE APP
THE APP

GardenLife is a garden blog app that allows users to connect with eachother and share their gardening journey. The Gardenlife app makes it easier to connect with other gardeners and help learn more about gardening.

THE EXPERIENCE

Gardeners are in need of a easlier way to communicate and be able to share and connect with other gardeners for advice and to connect with friends. This app makes it convenient for gardeners to stay connected with the gardening community.
