PARALLAX WEBSITE
CONCEPT

The concept for this project was to create a parallax website for a adventure company. I wanted to show a graphic/illustrative side to website design to make it more interesting and fun.

STYLE

The style is illustrative and made with alot of strong graphics to help give it a bold look even though it is very minimal. The style and colours also help create a calming scene for the viewer.
