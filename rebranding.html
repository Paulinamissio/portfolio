JC Robinson and Sons
Rebranding

JC Robinson & Sons is a heating and air conditioning company located on 1726 Bank Street here in Ottawa. JC Robinson & Sons has built an excellent customer base, made up of very loyal customers due to the family vibe.

Final Product

After various iterations, the final logo was vectorized and full guidelines were created. They include colour, typeface, correct and incorrect use, and possible applications.

The first symbol, the orange flame, represents the J in "JC Robinson and Sons" and their heating services. The second symbol, the blue snowflake, represents their air conditioning services.
