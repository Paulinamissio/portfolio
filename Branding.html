Echol
Branding, Stationary

I helped Echol, a new small business based in San diego, California with their logo and brand identity. The client wanted a fresh modern look with a korean feel.

Sketching Phase

Starting any project, I like to sketch out whatever comes to mind of the subject. I was inspired by their korean background and the current design trends.

Final Design

The final design was kept loose to help give it a more modern look. I wanted the logo to represent their artistry and modern korean food.
