Echol
Branding, Stationary

Echol is a new Korean restaurant based in San Diego, California. They wanted a fresh new logo that better represents who they are.

Concept

The concept was to incorportate elements of modern design with a touch of Korean style. The logo was hand drawn to help give it more of a fun element. The little elements reperesent food elements used in Korean cuisine.
