Echol Branding
OBJECTIVE

The objective of this project was to create a new and fresh logo for my client. My work was to create a fresh new logo along with other stationary items.

BACKGROUND

Echol is a Korean inspired restaurant based in San Diego, California. The company really wanted a warm, playful logo that would better represent their new updated restaurant.

CONCEPT

The concept for this logo was to incorporate elements of modern design and retro elements with a touch of korean style. I also hand drew their logos font to help make it more unique to them.
