ISOMETRIC DRAWING
THEME

The theme of my isometric drawing was a build a bear workship that invloved the steps to making your new furry friend. Each step shows the process of picking out a bear, getting it stuffed and picking your new friend up.

STYLE

I used pastel colours to show off my style and also add to the childish look I wanted for the drawing. The font used is to also help give it a more finished look.
